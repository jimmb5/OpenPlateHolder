How it works

Print. Mount. Snap.

Three steps from zero to a plate you can swap with one hand, in any weather.

01

Print the holder

Download the STL and slice it in your favourite slicer. No supports needed, flat on the bed. Done in under 2 hours.

02

Press in the magnet

Press your 20 mm magnet into the recessed pocket. Add a drop of superglue for a permanent, clean fit.

03

Stick it to the car

Attach the holder to the bumper with double-sided tape. Stick the metal counterpart to your plate — done.

04

Swap in seconds

Pull, swap, snap. Change plates in under five seconds, no tools, no screws, no stress.

Customize it

Your magnet. Your size.

Every dimension is a variable. Got a different magnet? Change one number. Wider plate? Change one number.

What you need

Four parts. That's it.

Magnets

20 mm × 8 mm disc magnets. Strong neodymium recommended. (STL pre-sized for these.)

Metal counterparts

Steel discs or plates mounted to the car. The holder (with magnet) attaches to the plate — the counterpart stays on the vehicle.

Superglue

A single drop keeps the magnet locked in the pocket permanently. Tight press-fit + glue = rock solid.

Double-sided tape

To stick the holder to the bumper. Gorilla Heavy Duty Mounting Tape is a proven choice.
